Before I Dev'd

As a kid, video games have always played a major part in my life. My granparents were cool enough to buy me a Nintendo Entertainment Syetem when I was a kid and I've never looked back since. I can recall playing Super Mario Bros. for the first time and even my first experience with renting Megaman when we moved into the house that my parents still live in. Video games have helped me foster friendships and bonds with people that I grew up with and also individuals that you may have met and connected with in a multi-player lobby. I so thankful for my experiences with video games. As I've gotten older and matured, video games have followed suit as well. The overall delivery of animations, pixels, colors and especially the music from the great developers and programmers has always had a hold on me. The music especially.

I would like to say that the first time I actually connected with a video game soundtrack would have to be my experience with was donkey kong country. It was a weekend rent and I recall making it to the water stage (super common for water stages to have great music.) and hearing the first few seconds of aquatic ambience by David Wise. Since that moment my love for video game soundtracks have grown so much. I decided to make a personal top 10 of all the VGM tracks that I've found myself connected to.

I hope you take time and listen to these tracks with an open mind and enjoy what you hear.
